ALLĒLŪIA. Laudātē Dominum in sānctīs eius; laudātē eum in firmāmentō virtūtis eius.
Laudātē eum in virtūtibus eius; laudātē eum secundum multitūdinem magnitūdinis eius.
Laudātē eum in sonō tubae; laudātē eum in psaltēriō, et citharā.
Laudātē eum in tympanō, et chorō; laudātē eum in chordīs, et organō.
Laudātē eum in cymbalīs bene sonantibus; laudātē eum in cymbalīs iūbilātiōnis.
Omnis spīritus laudet Dominum. Allēlūia.
